What is the problem being addressed

Currently advising students takes a considerable amount of time and with the explosion of student enrollment it is time consuming for the advisors. This leads to delayed response times for the students and advisors do not have adequate time to give detailed advisement.

What is your project idea and how will it work (what are its components etc.)

We believe there is a big opportunity to alleviate advisor`s time by automating the majority of questions being asked to them. We will be creating a product that can dynamically create a roadmap depending on the student`s needs and preferences (Full time student/preferred discipline, etc). This will give the student exactly what classes they need to take and in what order (Some classes have prerequisites). This will also help with negating user errors and help navigate them through their academic career and ensuring they only take the classes they need. With the support and guidance of multiple faculty members, this can revolutionize academic advisement and give students and advisors a product they can have confidence in.
